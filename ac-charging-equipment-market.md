<p><ul><li>Floor-standing Equipment</li><li>Wall-mounted Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC charging equipment for electric vehicles can be divided into two types: floor-standing equipment and wall-mounted equipment. Floor-standing equipment refers to charging stations that are installed on the ground and are typically found in public spaces such as parking lots or highways. They have a higher charging capacity and can accommodate multiple vehicles simultaneously. On the other hand, wall-mounted equipment is specifically designed for residential or private use. These charging stations are fixed to a wall and are more compact, offering convenience for EV owners to charge their vehicles at home or in private establishments.</p></p>
