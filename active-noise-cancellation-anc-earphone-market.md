<p><strong>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) Earphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Feedforward Active Noise Canceling</li><li>Feedback ANC</li><li>Hybrid ANC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) Earphone market encompasses various types. Feedforward Active Noise Canceling employs an external microphone to capture environmental sound, then generates an inverse sound wave to cancel it out. Feedback ANC utilizes an internal microphone to pick up the sound played by the earphone and perform cancellation. Hybrid ANC combines both feedforward and feedback methods for optimal noise reduction. These techniques enable ANC earphones to actively counteract ambient noise, enhancing the listening experience by delivering cleaner and more immersive sound to the user.</p></p>
