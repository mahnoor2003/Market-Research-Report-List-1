<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>In the advanced flat glass market, there are three main types: toughened glass, wired glass, and laminated glass. Toughened glass is heat-treated to increase its strength and resistance to breakage. Wired glass has a wire mesh embedded within it to enhance its fire resistance properties. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers of glass with an interlayer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) that enhances its safety features by holding the glass together when shattered. These different types of flat glass cater to various applications and provide improved safety and durability.</p></p>
