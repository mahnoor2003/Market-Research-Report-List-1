<p><p>Agricultural Automation Equipment market includes two main types: Driverless Equipment (AD) and Driver-Assisted Equipment (ADAS). Driverless Equipment refers to autonomous machines that can operate without human intervention, performing tasks such as planting, harvesting, and spraying. These machines are equipped with various sensors, cameras, and GPS technology to navigate and perform tasks accurately. On the other hand, Driver-Assisted Equipment requires human operators but provides assistance in tasks like steering, speed control, and precision farming. ADAS machines integrate advanced technologies such as sensors, cameras, and data analysis to enhance farm productivity and efficiency.</p></p>
