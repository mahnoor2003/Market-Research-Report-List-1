<p><p>The agriculture dyes and pigments market consists of two main types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are organic compounds that impart color to agricultural products such as textiles, crops, and animal feeds. They are soluble and easily penetrate the material, resulting in a vibrant color. Pigments, on the other hand, are insoluble particles that provide color through dispersion. They are mainly used in non-solvent-based applications such as coating and coloring fertilizers. Both dyes and pigments play a crucial role in enhancing the appearance and market value of agricultural products.</p></p>
