<p><p>The aircraft maintenance degreasers market consists of two types: water-based degreasers and solvent-based degreasers. Water-based degreasers are cleaning agents that are primarily composed of water and contain small amounts of surfactants and additives. They are effective in removing dirt, oil, and grease from aircraft surfaces while minimizing environmental impact. Solvent-based degreasers, on the other hand, are made up of chemicals that dissolve grease and oil. They are generally more potent than water-based degreasers but may pose higher health and environmental risks due to their volatile nature.</p></p>
