<p><p>Aircraft tilt sensors are used to measure the inclination or tilt of an aircraft relative to the Earth's horizon. There are two types of tilt sensors in the market: analog type and digital type. Analog tilt sensors provide a continuous output voltage or current proportional to the tilt angle, allowing for precise measurement. On the other hand, digital tilt sensors provide a discrete output, typically in the form of digital signals or numerical data, providing accurate readings and easy integration with digital systems. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications within the aircraft industry.</p></p>
