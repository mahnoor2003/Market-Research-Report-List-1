<p><p>Airport metal detectors are devices used by security personnel at airports to detect metallic objects carried by passengers. There are two main types: portable and fixed. Portable metal detectors are handheld devices that can be easily carried around and used by security personnel to scan individuals in different locations within the airport premises. On the other hand, fixed metal detectors are permanently installed at specific points, such as entry points or security checkpoints, and are used to screen passengers and their belongings as they pass through. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring airport security and detecting potential threats.</p></p>
