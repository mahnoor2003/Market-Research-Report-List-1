<p><p>The alkali-free glass substrate market consists of two main types: alkali-free aluminosilicate glass and alkali-free borosilicate glass. Alkali-free aluminosilicate glass is a type of glass that is composed mainly of aluminum, silicon, and oxygen, and it is known for its high-performance capabilities, including excellent chemical resistance and thermal stability. On the other hand, alkali-free borosilicate glass is composed mainly of boron, silicon, and oxygen, and it offers similar advantages as aluminosilicate glass, including high chemical resistance and low thermal expansion. Both types of glass substrates are widely used in various industries, including electronics, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
