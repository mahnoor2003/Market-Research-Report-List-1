<p><p>Aluminium recycling refers to the process of collecting, reprocessing, and reusing aluminium materials that have reached the end of their useful life. The increasing awareness about environmental issues, stringent regulations on waste management, and the need for sustainable practices are driving the demand for aluminium recycling. With the growing focus on circular economy principles, more companies and individuals are recognizing the value of recycling aluminium. The adoption of advanced technologies and processes in recycling facilities is also improving the efficiency and effectiveness of aluminium recycling.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing the emergence of innovative recycling methods like closed-loop recycling, which involves recycling aluminium scrap within the same industry. This approach allows for the retention of the material's value and reduces the dependence on primary aluminium production.</p><p>According to the provided information, the aluminium recycling market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 2.40% during the forecasted period.
