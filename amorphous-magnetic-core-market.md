<p><p>An amorphous magnetic core is a type of magnetic core material that is formed by rapidly cooling a molten alloy of iron, boron, and silicon. This rapid cooling process prevents the formation of a crystalline structure, resulting in an amorphous or non-crystalline material. Amorphous magnetic cores possess unique magnetic properties such as low magnetic losses, high magnetic permeability, and high saturation flux density. These characteristics make them highly desirable in various applications such as power transformers, inductors, and electric motors.</p><p>The future outlook for the amorphous magnetic core market is quite promising.
