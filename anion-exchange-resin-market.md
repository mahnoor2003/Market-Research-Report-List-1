<p><p>Anion exchange resin is categorized into two types: strong base and weak base. Strong base anion resin possesses a high capacity to remove negatively charged ions from water, making it effective for applications requiring a high level of purification. On the other hand, weak base anion resin has a lower capacity and is more suitable for applications where a moderate level of purification is needed. Both types of resins play a crucial role in water treatment processes, each offering different strengths and capabilities for specific purification requirements.</p></p>
