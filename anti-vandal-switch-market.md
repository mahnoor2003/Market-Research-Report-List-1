Standard Anti-vandal Switch: This type is durable and features a mechanical push-button design, making it suitable for various applications. It provides a tactile feedback when pressed.</p><p>2. Capacitive Anti-vandal Switch: Utilizing a touch-sensitive technology, this switch operates without physical contact. It is highly reliable, offers customization options, and is ideal for environments where hygiene and water resistance are important.</p><p>3. Piezoelectric Anti-vandal Switch: It uses piezoelectric materials to generate an electrical response when pressure is applied. This switch is known for its long lifespan, resistance to harsh conditions, and suitability for applications that require high durability and reliability.</p></p>
