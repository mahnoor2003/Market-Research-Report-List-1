<p><p>The Architectural Acoustic Insulation Glass market includes different types of glass products that provide sound insulation in buildings. Hollow glass consists of two or more layers of glass with a hollow space in between, which helps reduce noise transmission. Vacuum glass has a vacuum layer between glass panes, offering enhanced soundproofing capabilities. Laminated glass incorporates a layer of plastic between glass layers, providing both sound insulation and added safety features. These glass types cater to the diverse needs of architectural projects aiming to minimize noise pollution.</p></p>
