<p><p>The Automatic Discrete Chemical Analyzer market includes two main types: Flow Cell Based System and Direct Read System. A Flow Cell Based System utilizes a flow cell chamber to mix and analyze the chemical samples, providing accurate and efficient results. On the other hand, a Direct Read System directly measures the sample without requiring a separate mixing chamber. Both systems are widely used in various industries for automated and precise chemical analysis.</p></p>
