<p><p>The market for automatic pipetting workstations can be classified into two types: single-channel automated pipetting workstation and multi-channel automated pipetting workstation. A single-channel workstation allows for the dispensing and aspiration of liquids using a single pipette, increasing accuracy and reducing variability. On the other hand, a multi-channel workstation comprises multiple pipettes that can simultaneously handle multiple samples, increasing efficiency in high-throughput applications. Both types cater to the automation of pipetting tasks, offering different levels of throughput and flexibility depending on the user's requirements.</p></p>
