<p><p>The automotive body sealing system market encompasses various types of sealing materials such as EPDM, TPE, and PVC. EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) is a synthetic rubber known for its excellent weather resistance and durability. TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) combines the qualities of rubber and plastic, offering good flexibility and easy processing. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is a durable thermoplastic material commonly used in automotive applications due to its low cost and chemical resistance. These materials play a crucial role in preventing water, dust, and noise infiltration, ensuring the integrity and comfort of automotive bodies.</p></p>
