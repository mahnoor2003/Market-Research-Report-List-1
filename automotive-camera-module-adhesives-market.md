<p><p>The automotive camera module adhesives market is divided into two types: UV curable type and non-UV curable type. UV curable adhesives are formulated to cure or harden when exposed to ultraviolet light. These adhesives offer fast curing times, high bond strength, and excellent adhesion to various materials. On the other hand, non-UV curable adhesives do not require UV light for curing. They offer a wide range of curing methods, including heat, moisture, or chemical reaction, providing versatility in different manufacturing processes.</p></p>
