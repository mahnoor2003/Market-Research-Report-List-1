<p><p>Automotive cooling fan control modules are essential components in regulating the operation of cooling fans in vehicles. There are two main types: mechanical cooling fan control modules and electric cooling fan control modules. Mechanical control modules use a temperature-sensitive switch to engage or disengage the cooling fan based on the engine temperature, while electric control modules rely on electronic signals from the engine control unit to regulate the fan speed. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining optimum engine temperature and preventing overheating, ensuring efficient vehicle performance and durability.</p></p>
