<p><p>The automotive cooling fans market consists of two main types: electric fans and mechanical fans. Electric fans are powered by electricity and are controlled by the engine's computer system. They are more efficient and provide better cooling compared to mechanical fans. On the other hand, mechanical fans are belt-driven by the engine and do not require electricity.
