<p><p>The automotive mufflers market can be classified into two main types: absorptive mufflers and reactive mufflers. Absorptive mufflers are designed to reduce noise levels by utilizing sound-absorbing materials, such as fiberglass or foam, to dampen the sound waves produced by the engine. On the other hand, reactive mufflers use a series of chambers and tubes to reflect and cancel out sound waves, thereby reducing noise. Both types play a crucial role in minimizing vehicle noise and ensuring a more comfortable driving experience for passengers.</p></p>
