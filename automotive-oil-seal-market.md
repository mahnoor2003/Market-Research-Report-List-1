<p><strong>The Automotive Oil Seal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HNBR/NBR Oil Seal</li><li>ACM/AEM Oil Seal</li><li>FKM/FPM Oil Seal</li><li>PTFE Oil Seal</li><li>Other Oil Seal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive oil seal market is divided into various types. HNBR/NBR oil seal, ACM/AEM oil seal, FKM/FPM oil seal, PTFE oil seal, and other oil seals are the major segments. HNBR/NBR oil seals are made of nitrile rubber and provide excellent resistance to oil and heat. ACM/AEM oil seals are made of acrylic rubber and exhibit high resistance to ozone and weathering. FKM/FPM oil seals are made of fluorocarbon rubber and offer high resistance to chemicals and extreme temperatures. PTFE oil seals are made of polytetrafluoroethylene and ensure low friction and high chemical resistance. Other oil seals include various types like silicone oil seals, polyurethane oil seals, etc.</p></p>
