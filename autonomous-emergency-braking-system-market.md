Low Speed-City AEB Systems focus on preventing accidents in urban areas with slower speeds, such as cities or residential areas. Pedestrian-VRU AEB Systems are specifically developed to detect and avoid collisions with pedestrians or other vulnerable road users, such as cyclists or motorcyclists.</p></p>
