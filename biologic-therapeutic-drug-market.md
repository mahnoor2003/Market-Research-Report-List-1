<p><p>The biologic therapeutic drug market consists of three main types: monoclonal antibodies, therapeutic proteins, and vaccines. Monoclonal antibodies are lab-created proteins that target specific cells or molecules in the body to treat various diseases. Therapeutic proteins are large molecules produced in living cells that can replace or enhance the function of deficient proteins in the body. Vaccines stimulate the immune system to prevent or treat infectious diseases by introducing harmless components of the pathogen into the body. These different types of drugs play a crucial role in addressing various medical conditions and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
