<p><p>The bleached specialty Kraft papers market consists of three main types: smooth-finished grade, machine-glazed grade, and machine-finished grade. Smooth-finished grade papers have a flat and smooth surface, providing an excellent base for printing and coating applications. Machine-glazed grade papers have a glossy and shiny surface due to the glazing process, making them suitable for applications that require a polished appearance. Machine-finished grade papers have a rougher surface compared to smooth-finished grade papers but are still suitable for printing and converting applications while offering higher bulk and stiffness.</p></p>
