<p><p>The COB (Chip-on-Board) Light Emitting Diode market consists of two main types: Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) and Inorganic Light Emitting Diode (ILED). OLED technology uses thin organic layers to emit light, which allows for flexibility and better color reproduction. In contrast, ILEDs use inorganic semiconductors, such as gallium nitride, to emit light, providing better power efficiency and longer lifespan. Both types have their advantages and are used in various applications, including display screens, lighting fixtures, and automotive lighting.</p></p>
