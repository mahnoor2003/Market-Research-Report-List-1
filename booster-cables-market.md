<p><p>Booster cables, also known as jumper cables or jump leads, are electrical cables with clamps used to help start a vehicle that has a discharged or flat battery. They provide a temporary connection between the dead battery and a fully charged battery in another vehicle, allowing the engine to start. Booster cables are a popular and widely used tool for emergency situations, especially in the automotive industry, where they are an essential part of every vehicle's emergency kit.</p><p>The current outlook of the booster cables market is positive, with steady growth expected in the coming years. The increasing demand for vehicles globally, along with the rising number of road accidents and breakdowns, fuels the market's growth. Additionally, the growing awareness among vehicle owners about the importance of being prepared for emergencies further boosts the market.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements have led to the development of advanced booster cables, such as smart jumper cables equipped with safety features like reverse polarity protection and short-circuit protection.
