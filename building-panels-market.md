<p><p>The building panels market consists of various types of panels that serve different purposes in the construction industry. Concrete panels are sturdy and commonly used in high-rise construction. Vacuum insulated panels (VIP) are highly efficient in terms of insulation and are primarily used in projects that require energy conservation. Structural insulated panels (SIP) are a combination of insulation and structural support, providing strength and energy efficiency. Wood panels are often used in residential and commercial buildings for their aesthetic appeal and versatility in design.</p></p>
