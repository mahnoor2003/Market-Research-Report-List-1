<p><p>Carboxymethyl Cellulose (CMC) is a cellulose derivative created through the chemical modification of cellulose with sodium hydroxide and chloroacetic acid. CMC finds extensive utilization in industries such as food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, personal care, oil drilling, paper processing, textiles, and many more.</p><p>The global market for Carboxymethyl Cellulose is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period. The rising demand for processed and convenience food products is one of the significant factors driving the market growth. CMC is extensively used as a thickener, stabilizer, and emulsifier in various food products such as sauces, dressings, ice creams, and baked goods. Additionally, the pharmaceutical industry's increasing use of CMC in tablet formulations and suspensions further contributes to the market's growth.</p><p>The Carboxymethyl Cellulose Market is also boosted by the growing demand for low-fat and low-calorie diet products, where CMC acts as a fat replacer and provides the desired texture and taste.
