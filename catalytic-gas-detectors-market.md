<p><p>Catalytic gas detectors are used for identifying the presence of flammable gases in various industries. Point detectors are compact devices installed at specific locations to monitor the concentration of gases at a certain point, detecting potential hazards in real-time. Open path detectors use infrared technology for identifying gases across a larger area, continuously monitoring gas levels in a specific path. Both detectors are essential for ensuring safety and alerting personnel to any potential gas leaks or hazardous situations in industrial environments.</p></p>
