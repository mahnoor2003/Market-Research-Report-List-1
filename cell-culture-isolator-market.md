<p><p>The Cell Culture Isolator market includes two major types: Standard Isolator and Modular Isolator. Standard Isolator is a self-contained unit designed for conducting cell culture experiments under sterile conditions. It provides a controlled environment with minimal physical interaction. On the other hand, Modular Isolator consists of connecting chambers that can be customized and expanded according to specific laboratory requirements. It offers flexibility and scalability in terms of experimental setups. These isolators are essential tools for researchers to prevent contamination and maintain optimal conditions for cell growth and experimentation.</p></p>
