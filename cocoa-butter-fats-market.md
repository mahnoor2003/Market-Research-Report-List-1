<p><p>The cocoa butter fats market can be categorized into three main types: cocoa butter equivalents, cocoa butter substitutes, and others. Cocoa butter equivalents are fats derived from non-cocoa sources that have similar properties to cocoa butter and can be used as a substitute. Cocoa butter substitutes, on the other hand, are fats that are completely non-cocoa based and are used as replacements for cocoa butter. The "others" category includes fats that do not fall under the previous two categories but still contribute to the overall cocoa butter fats market.</p></p>
