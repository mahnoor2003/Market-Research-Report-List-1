<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor Market comprises two types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type wheel speed sensors utilize the Hall effect to generate an output voltage proportional to the wheel's rotational speed. On the other hand, Magnetic Electric Type sensors use magnetic induction to measure wheel speed. Both types of sensors are commonly used in commercial vehicles to accurately detect wheel speed and provide essential information for crucial vehicle systems like anti-lock braking systems (ABS) and traction control systems (TCS).</p></p>
