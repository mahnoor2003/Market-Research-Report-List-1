<p><p>Compression packing materials are used to create a seal in rotating equipment, preventing leakage. Carbon packing is made from carbon fibers, offering excellent self-lubricating properties. PTFE packing is a versatile option suitable for various applications due to its chemical resistance. Graphite packing is known for its high-temperature performance and minimal friction. Asbestos packing was formerly popular but is now less favored due to health concerns. PTFE blends packing combines PTFE with other materials for enhanced properties. Other compression packing materials include aramid fibers, ceramic fibers, and synthetic fibers, each with unique advantages.</p></p>
