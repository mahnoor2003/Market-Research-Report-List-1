<p><p>Coroplast sheets, also known as corrugated plastic sheets or corriboard, are made from a lightweight and durable plastic material called polypropylene. They are characterized by their twin-wall structure with a series of parallel hollow channels that provide strength and rigidity. Coroplast sheets offer various advantages such as weather resistance, chemical resistance, easy fabrication, and recyclability, making them suitable for versatile applications.</p><p>The global coroplast sheets market has been experiencing steady growth and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period. The market is primarily driven by the increasing demand for lightweight and environmentally sustainable materials across industries such as packaging, construction, automotive, advertising, and electronics.</p><p>In the packaging industry, coroplast sheets are widely being used for signage, displays, protective packaging, and transit packaging due to their excellent tensile and tear strength. The growing e-commerce sector is also contributing to the demand for coroplast sheets as they are an ideal choice for shipping and packaging applications.</p><p>In the construction industry, coroplast sheets are being used as temporary protection sheets, wall panels, floor protection, roofing, and insulation due to their lightweight nature, durability, and ability to withstand harsh weather conditions. The increasing number of construction projects worldwide is driving the demand for coroplast sheets in this sector.</p><p>The advertising industry is witnessing a surge in the use of coroplast sheets for outdoor signage, banners, and promotional displays due to their weather resistance and flexibility. Moreover, the automotive industry is also adopting coroplast sheets for applications such as interior trim, door panels, and underbody shields, owing to their lightweight and cost-effective properties.</p><p>The coroplast sheets market is characterized by intense competition among key players, leading to continuous product innovation and development.
