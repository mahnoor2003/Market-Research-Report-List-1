<p><p>The corrugated paper market consists of two main types: single corrugated cardboard and double corrugated cardboard. Single corrugated cardboard is made up of one layer of fluted paper securely glued between two flat layers, providing moderate strength and flexibility. On the other hand, double corrugated cardboard has an additional layer of fluted paper inserted in between the two flat layers, making it stronger and more rigid. These different market types cater to various packaging needs, with double corrugated cardboard being ideal for heavier or fragile items and single corrugated cardboard suitable for lightweight products.</p></p>
