<p><p>Cyclic Olefin Prefillable Injection Device (COPID) market consists of three major types of materials: Crystal Clear Polymer (CCP), Cyclic Olefin Polymer (COP), and Cyclic Olefin Copolymer (COC). CCP refers to a high-purity material that offers excellent transparency and is ideal for drug delivery systems. COP is a stable and robust material that provides enhanced mechanical properties and gas barrier characteristics. COC is a polymer blend that combines the advantageous properties of both CCP and COP, offering excellent clarity, moisture barrier, and chemical resistance for prefilled injection devices.</p></p>
