<p><strong>The Diesel Automotive Injector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Throttle Body Injection</li><li>Multipoint Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li><li>Common Rail Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diesel Automotive Injector Market consists of four types: Throttle Body Injection, Multipoint Injection, Direct Injection, and Common Rail Injection. Throttle Body Injection delivers fuel to the intake manifold through a single injector, while Multipoint Injection uses multiple injectors to deliver fuel individually to each cylinder. Direct Injection injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber, resulting in better fuel efficiency and performance. Common Rail Injection operates by storing fuel under high pressure in a common rail, enabling precise fuel delivery to each cylinder. Each type of injection system offers unique advantages and caters to different vehicle requirements.</p></p>
