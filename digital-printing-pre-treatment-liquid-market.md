<p><p>Digital Printing Pre-treatment Liquid is a chemical solution used in the pre-treatment process for digital printing on textiles. This liquid is applied to fabric prior to printing to improve the color vibrancy, durability, and washability of the printed design. It helps in better color penetration into the fabric and prevents ink bleeding or smudging. The pre-treatment liquid also acts as a binding agent for the ink, ensuring adhesion to the fabric surface and enhancing the overall print quality.</p><p>The global Digital Printing Pre-treatment Liquid Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for digital printing in textile applications is one of the major factors driving market growth.
