<p><ul><li>DLP Projectors</li><li>LCD Projectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital projectors for education are categorized into two main types: DLP (Digital Light Processing) projectors and LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) projectors. DLP projectors use tiny mirrors to reflect light and create images, resulting in superior color accuracy and contrast. On the other hand, LCD projectors utilize liquid crystal panels to project images, offering vibrant colors and high resolution. Both types have their advantages: DLP projectors possess better black levels and smooth motion, while LCD projectors excel in brightness and sharpness. The choice between the two depends on the specific needs and preferences of educational institutions.</p></p>
