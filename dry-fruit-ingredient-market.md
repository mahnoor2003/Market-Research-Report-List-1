Moreover, the rise in vegan and vegetarian diets is creating new opportunities for the market, as dried fruits serve as an excellent natural alternative for meat-based products.</p><p>Furthermore, dry fruit ingredients have a longer shelf life compared to fresh fruits, which makes them a convenient choice for manufacturers and consumers.
