<p><p>The elastomeric foam rubber market can be classified into different types based on the materials used. NBR foam, also known as nitrile rubber foam, is widely used due to its excellent resistance to oil and fuel. EPDM foam, or ethylene propylene diene monomer foam, is known for its excellent weathering resistance and is commonly used in outdoor applications. Other types of elastomeric foam rubber include CR foam (chloroprene rubber foam) and PVC/NBR foam (polyvinyl chloride/nitrile butadiene rubber foam), among others. These different types cater to various industries and applications, offering specific benefits and characteristics.</p></p>
