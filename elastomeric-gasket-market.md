Rubber gaskets are made from various types of rubber materials, such as natural rubber or synthetic rubber, and they have excellent resistance to heat, chemicals, and weathering effects. On the other hand, thermoplastic elastomer gaskets are manufactured using a combination of plastic and rubber polymers, providing enhanced flexibility, durability, and resistance to temperature variations. Both types of gaskets are widely used in industries like automotive, electrical, and construction for secure sealing applications.</p></p>
