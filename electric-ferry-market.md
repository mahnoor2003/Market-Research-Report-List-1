<p><p>The electric ferry market can be categorized into two main types: Pure Electric and Hybrid Power. </p><p>Pure Electric ferries are powered solely by electricity. They use rechargeable battery systems, eliminating the need for fossil fuels and reducing emissions. These ferries are considered more environmentally friendly and have lower operating costs in the long run.</p><p>Hybrid Power ferries, on the other hand, combine electric propulsion systems with traditional fuel-based engines. They can switch between these power sources depending on the operational requirements, providing flexibility and potentially longer cruising distances. This type can offer a transition solution towards full electrification for ferry operators.</p></p>
