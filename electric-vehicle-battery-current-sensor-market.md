<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Battery Current Sensor market consists of different types, including Hall Based Current Sensor, Shunt Based Current Sensor, and Others. The Hall Based Current Sensor utilizes the Hall effect to measure the electric current passing through a conductor. The Shunt Based Current Sensor, on the other hand, measures the current through a low resistance shunt by sensing the voltage drop across it. The "Others" category encompasses any other types of current sensors used in electric vehicle batteries, which may employ different measurement techniques or technologies.</p></p>
