<p><p>Electrical and electronics adhesives find application in various areas such as conformal coating, encapsulation, surface mounting, and wire tacking. Conformal coating involves applying a thin protective layer on electronic circuits to enhance their resistance to environmental factors. Encapsulation is the process of enclosing and protecting electronic components within a protective material to prevent damage. Surface mounting involves attaching electronic components directly onto a printed circuit board for efficient assembly. Wire tacking is the process of securing wires to ensure proper electrical connections. These applications play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and longevity of electrical and electronics systems.</p></p>
