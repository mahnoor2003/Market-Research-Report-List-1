<p><p>The electromagnetic wave shield film market consists of two main types: metal-mesh film and coating film. Metal-mesh film is made up of a fine mesh of conductive metal wires that block electromagnetic waves while still allowing visibility. This type of film offers excellent shielding properties and is commonly used in electronic devices and displays. Coating film, on the other hand, is a thin layer of conductive material applied to a surface. It provides effective electromagnetic wave shielding by reflecting or absorbing the radiation. Coating film is often utilized in buildings, automotive, and aerospace industries for its versatility and ease of application.</p></p>
