<p><p>The electronic adhesives market consists of various types such as optical adhesive, liquid encapsulant, SMT adhesive, potting compound, structural adhesive, and others. Optical adhesive is used for bonding optical components in electronic devices. Liquid encapsulant provides protection and insulation for electronic circuits. SMT adhesive is specifically designed for surface mount technology applications. Potting compound is used to encase electronic components and provide protection against moisture and contaminants. Structural adhesive is used for bonding large electronic components. The "others" category includes adhesives used for various specific applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
