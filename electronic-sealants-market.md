<p><ul><li>Light Curing</li><li>Thermal Curing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic sealants are used to create a protective barrier in electronic devices, preventing the penetration of moisture, dust, and other external elements. The market can be categorized into three types: light curing, thermal curing, and others. Light curing sealants utilize ultraviolet light to cure and form a strong bond, offering quick and precise sealing. Thermal curing sealants, on the other hand, require heat to cure and provide excellent adhesion properties in extreme conditions. The remaining category includes sealants that use different techniques or combinations to deliver effective sealing and protection solutions for electronic components.</p></p>
