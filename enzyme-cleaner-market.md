<p><p>Enzyme cleaner is a type of cleaning solution that utilizes enzymes to break down and eliminate stains, odors, and organic matter from various surfaces. Enzymes are biodegradable proteins that act as catalysts, speeding up chemical reactions to break down complex molecules into simpler forms. In the case of enzyme cleaners, these enzymes target specific substances such as proteins, fats, cellulose, and starch, ensuring thorough cleaning without causing any harm to the surface or the environment.</p><p>The enzyme cleaner market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the increasing awareness about the harmful effects of chemical-based cleaning products. Consumers are now opting for eco-friendly and safe cleaning solutions, leading to a surge in demand for enzyme cleaners. Moreover, the hospitality industry, healthcare sector, and commercial cleaning services are also embracing enzyme cleaners due to their effectiveness in eliminating tough stains and odors.</p><p>Furthermore, the enzyme cleaner market is experiencing a shift towards innovative product offerings.
