<p><p>The enzyme-linked immunoassay (ELISA) kit market offers various types of kits for different applications. Sandwich ELISA kits are used for detecting and quantifying an antigen of interest by sandwiching it between two antibodies. Indirect ELISA kits detect antibodies in a sample by using a labeled secondary antibody. Competitive ELISA kits measure the competition between the antigen in the sample and a known labeled antigen. Other types of ELISA kits include direct ELISA and reverse ELISA, which have slight variations in their approach but serve the same purpose of detecting antigens or antibodies in samples.</p></p>
