<p><p>The exhaust catalyst market can be categorized into two types: tightly coupled catalyst and bottom tray catalyst. Tightly coupled catalysts are designed to be placed close to the engine, allowing for faster response times and better control of emissions. On the other hand, bottom tray catalysts are positioned at the bottom of the vehicle, near the exhaust system, in order to maximize the conversion of harmful pollutants. Both types of catalysts play a crucial role in reducing exhaust emissions and improving air quality.</p></p>
