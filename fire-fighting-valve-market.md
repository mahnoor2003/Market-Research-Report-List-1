<p><p>The fire-fighting valve market comprises various types of valves that are used in fire protection systems. Butterfly valves are quarter-turn valves with a circular disk that controls flow. UL/FM gate valves meet specific fire safety standards. Pressure reducing valves regulate water pressure. Alarm check valves prevent water backflow. Deluge valves rapidly release large amounts of water. Ball valves offer quick shut-off functions. Gate valves control water flow with a gate mechanism. Other valve types also exist in this market, offering various functionalities for fire-fighting applications.</p></p>
