<p><p>Fireproof insulation refers to a type of insulation material that can withstand high temperatures and prevent the spread of fire. There are several market types available, including glass wool, stone wool, plastic foam, and others. Glass wool insulation is made from recycled glass and is known for its excellent thermal and acoustic properties. Stone wool insulation is produced from volcanic rock and offers superior fire resistance and sound absorption. Plastic foam insulation, on the other hand, is made from synthetic materials and provides good thermal insulation but may release toxic fumes when exposed to fire. Other types of fireproof insulation include cellulose and mineral wool.</p></p>
