<p><p>Flat panel display products refer to a range of technologies used for visual displays. PDPs (plasma display panels) use tiny cells of ionized gases to create images, while VFDs (vacuum fluorescent displays) use filaments to emit light through a vacuum. FEDs (field emission displays) use electrons emitted from tiny tips to illuminate pixels. LEDs (light-emitting diodes) use semiconductors to emit light, while OLEDs (organic light-emitting diodes) use organic compounds. LCDs (liquid crystal displays) use liquid crystals to manipulate light. Each technology has its own unique qualities, applications, and market segments. Other flat panel display technologies also exist, serving varying niche markets.</p></p>
