FEP is commonly used in electrical insulation and cookware due to its nonstick and high-temperature properties. Other types of fluoropolymer materials include Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) and Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene (ETFE), which find applications in various industries like automotive, electronics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
