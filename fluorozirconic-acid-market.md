<p><p>Fluorozirconic acid is available in two market types: industrial grade and reagent grade. It is generally less pure and has a wider range of impurities, suitable for industrial applications. On the other hand, reagent grade fluorozirconic acid is highly pure and free from impurities. It is used in laboratories and research facilities for analytical purposes, as a reagent, or in the preparation of other chemicals.</p></p>
