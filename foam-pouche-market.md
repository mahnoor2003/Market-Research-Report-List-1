<p><p>The Foam Pouch market is categorized into different types based on the type of polyethylene used. Middle Density Polyethylene (MDPE) foam pouches offer a good balance between strength and flexibility. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) foam pouches are highly flexible and provide excellent cushioning. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) foam pouches have enhanced tear resistance and are suitable for heavy-duty applications. These different types cater to diverse packaging needs and provide unique properties for various industries.</p></p>
