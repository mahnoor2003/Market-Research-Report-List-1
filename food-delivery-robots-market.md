<p><p>Food delivery robots can be categorized into two main types: ground robots and aerial drones. Ground robots are designed to navigate and make deliveries on land. They typically move on wheels or tracks and are capable of carrying food packages over short distances within a limited radius. On the other hand, aerial drones are unmanned aircraft that use rotors to fly. They are capable of delivering food by air, allowing for faster and more efficient deliveries over longer distances compared to ground robots.</p></p>
