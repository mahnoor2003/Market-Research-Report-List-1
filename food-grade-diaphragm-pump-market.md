<p><p>The food-grade diaphragm pump market consists of two main types: Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD) and Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps (EODD). AODDs are powered by compressed air and are commonly used in food processing industries due to their ability to handle viscous and abrasive materials. EODDs, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and offer higher efficiency and precision. Both types are specifically designed to meet the stringent requirements of the food industry, ensuring safe and hygienic pumping of food products.</p></p>
