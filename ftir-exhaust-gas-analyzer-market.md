<p><p>FTIR (Fourier Transform Infrared) Exhaust Gas Analyzer market types include dilute measurement type and direct measurement type. The dilute measurement type involves the sampling of exhaust gas by diluting it with air, allowing for accurate measurements of various pollutants. It is suitable for lower exhaust gas concentrations. On the other hand, the direct measurement type directly analyzes the undiluted exhaust gas, providing real-time measurements for higher exhaust gas concentrations. Both types are utilized in different scenarios depending on the required accuracy of measurement and concentration levels of pollutants.</p></p>
