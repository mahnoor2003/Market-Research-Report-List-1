<p><p>Fused silica microlens array market consists of two main types: aspherical and spherical microlens arrays. Aspherical microlens arrays feature non-uniform curvature and enable efficient light focusing and better optical performance, making them suitable for applications like microscopes and laser scanning systems. On the other hand, spherical microlens arrays have a uniform curvature and are used in imaging devices such as cameras and projectors. Both types play a crucial role in improving image quality and enhancing optical systems' performance across various industries.</p></p>
