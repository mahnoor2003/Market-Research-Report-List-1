<p><p>The gas vent market encompasses two main types: single tailpipe type and double tailpipe type. On the other hand, the double tailpipe type features a dual-pipe system for expelling gas. This is typically seen in larger vehicles or high-performance cars, where the exhaust system requires increased capacity for releasing larger amounts of gas or achieving a sportier aesthetic.</p></p>
