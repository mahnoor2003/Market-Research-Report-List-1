<p><p>Gasoline fuel additives are diverse in types and serve various purposes. Deposit control additives prevent the formation of harmful deposits in engines, enhancing fuel efficiency. Cetane improvers increase the cetane number, improving combustion and reducing emissions in diesel engines. Lubricity improvers reduce friction and wear between engine components. Antioxidants inhibit the oxidation process, extending fuel shelf life. Anti-icing additives prevent fuel line freezing in cold temperatures. Octane improvers enhance the octane rating, promoting smoother engine operation. Other additives include corrosion inhibitors, demulsifiers, and metal deactivators, which protect engines from damage caused by contaminants and moisture.</p></p>
