<p><p>The Gel-Filled Fiber Cable market consists of two main types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode fiber cables. Single-Mode cables transmit light signals over longer distances and are ideal for long-haul telecommunications applications. They have a narrow diameter core, allowing only a single pathway for light to travel. On the other hand, Multi-Mode cables have a larger core diameter and are designed for shorter distances within data centers or local area networks. They allow multiple light signals to be transmitted simultaneously through different pathways, enabling faster data transfer rates over shorter distances.</p></p>
