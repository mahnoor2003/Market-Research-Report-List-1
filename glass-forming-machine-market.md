<p><ul><li>Blow & Blow</li><li>Press & Blow</li><li>Narrow Neck Press and Blow (NNPB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Forming Machine Market comprises various types, including Blow & Blow, Press & Blow, and Narrow Neck Press and Blow (NNPB). Blow & Blow is a technique where the glass container is initially blown vertically and then shaped into the desired form. Press & Blow involves an initial pressing of the glass into a parison, which is then blown to its final shape. NNPB is a variation of Press & Blow specifically used for producing narrow-neck containers like bottles or jars. These techniques help in efficiently manufacturing different glass containers.</p></p>
