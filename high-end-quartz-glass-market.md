<p><p>The high-end quartz glass market comprises two main types: quartz tube and quartz rod. Quartz tubes are cylindrical structures made from quartz glass and find applications in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, telecommunications, and lighting. They are commonly used as furnaces, heaters, and other high-temperature applications due to their excellent thermal properties. On the other hand, quartz rods are solid cylindrical structures also made from quartz glass and are utilized in optical applications, such as fiber optics and lenses, due to their high clarity and superior light transmission capabilities.</p></p>
