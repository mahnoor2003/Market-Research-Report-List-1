<p><p>The high-pressure roll compactor market comprises two main types: briquetting and granulation. Briquetting involves compressing materials into briquettes, which are solid blocks with high density and reduced volume. This process is commonly used to create homogeneous fuel sources for industrial applications. On the other hand, granulation involves the formation of granules by compacting powder materials. This technique is widely used to produce pharmaceutical tablets or enhance the flow properties of powdered substances. Both briquetting and granulation play essential roles in improving material handling, transportation, and application processes for various industries.</p></p>
