<p><p>The industrial air pollution control solutions market consists of different types of systems that help reduce air pollution. Electrostatic precipitators (ESP) use an electric field to remove particles from the air. Flue gas desulfurization (FGD) systems remove sulfur dioxide from power plant emissions. Scrubbers use liquid to remove pollutants from gas streams. Selective catalytic reduction (SCR) systems use catalysts to convert harmful gases into harmless substances. Fabric filters remove particulate matter through the use of filters. These solutions play a crucial role in minimizing industrial air pollution and maintaining environmental standards.</p></p>
