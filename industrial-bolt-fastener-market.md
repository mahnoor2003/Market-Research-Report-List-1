<p><p>The industrial bolt market consists of different types of bolts used in various applications. Two common types are the half screw bolt and the full screw bolt. A half screw bolt is a fastener that has a partially threaded shank. It is commonly used in situations where it is necessary to secure two objects while allowing for some movement or adjustment. On the other hand, a full screw bolt has a fully threaded shank, providing maximum stability and resistance. The choice between these two types depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
