<p><p>Iron castings are widely used in various industries due to their durability and strength. The market for iron castings can be classified into three types: gray iron, ductile iron, and malleable iron. Gray iron is known for its high resistance to wear and excellent castability. Ductile iron possesses high tensile strength and is ideal for applications that require impact resistance. Malleable iron, on the other hand, is highly ductile, making it suitable for applications where deformation is required. These different types of iron castings cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
