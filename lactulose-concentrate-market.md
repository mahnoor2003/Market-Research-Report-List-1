<p><p>The Lactulose Concentrate market is categorized into two types: Chemical Synthesis and Enzymatic Method. Chemical Synthesis refers to the production of lactulose by using chemical reactions to convert lactose into lactulose. On the other hand, the Enzymatic Method involves the use of enzymes to catalyze the conversion of lactose into lactulose. Both methods are used in the market for producing lactulose concentrate, with chemical synthesis being the conventional method and the enzymatic method gaining popularity due to its eco-friendly nature.</p></p>
