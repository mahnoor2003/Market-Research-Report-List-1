<p><p>Li-ion batteries are widely used in the transportation sector due to their high energy density, long cycle life, and light weight. Various types of Li-ion batteries are available in the market. Lithium Cobalt Oxide (LiCoO2) batteries offer high energy density but have limited thermal stability. Lithium Manganese Oxide (LiMn2O4) batteries provide better thermal stability but have lower energy density. Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries offer long cycle life and excellent thermal stability. Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt Oxide (LiNMC) and Lithium Nickel Cobalt Aluminum Oxide (NCA) batteries have higher energy densities and power capabilities. Lithium Titanate (Li4Ti5O12) batteries are known for their high power and long cycle life.</p></p>
