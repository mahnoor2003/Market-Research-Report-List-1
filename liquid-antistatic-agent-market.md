<p><p>Liquid antistatic agent is a type of chemical additive used to reduce or eliminate static electricity on various surfaces, such as plastics, textiles, and electronics. The growing adoption of electronics in the automotive sector and the rising production of electronic components are major factors contributing to the market growth. Additionally, the increasing use of plastics in various industries and the need to reduce static charge generation in plastic materials further fuel market demand.</p><p>Moreover, the expanding textile industry, particularly in emerging economies, is driving the demand for liquid antistatic agents.
