Unlike traditional sanding, liquid sandpaper produces less dust and avoids the release of harmful particles into the air. <p><p>The liquid sandpaper market is divided into four types based on particle coarseness. Rough particle liquid sandpaper is ideal for heavy-duty sanding tasks, such as removing paint or varnish. Medium coarseness particle liquid sandpaper is suitable for general purpose sanding and preparing surfaces for painting. Fine particle liquid sandpaper provides a smoother finish and is used for delicate sanding and polishing tasks. Lastly, ultra-fine particle liquid sandpaper is designed for high-end finishing and produces a glossy and polished surface. These different types cater to various sanding needs and achieve different levels of surface smoothness.</p></p>
