<p><strong>The Liquified Natural Gas Carrier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-supporting Type</li><li>Film Type</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Liquified Natural Gas (LNG) carriers are classified into two types: self-supporting and film type. Self-supporting LNG carriers have spherical or prismatic tanks that are self-supporting structures and can maintain their shape even when empty. These carriers are built with steel plates and provide high safety and structural reliability. On the other hand, film type LNG carriers have membrane tanks made of thin metal sheets supported by insulation. These carriers are lighter, cheaper, and more flexible when it comes to tank shape design, but they require careful handling and maintenance.</p></p>
