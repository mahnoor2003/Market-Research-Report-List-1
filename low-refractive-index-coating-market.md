<p><p>Low refractive index coating is a type of coating that is applied to optical surfaces to reduce light reflection and increase light transmission. Low refractive index coatings are designed to have a refractive index lower than that of the substrate material, which allows for high transmission of light.</p><p>The market for low refractive index coatings is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
