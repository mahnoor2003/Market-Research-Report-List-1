Metal ceiling tiles are constructed using various metals such as aluminum or steel, offering a sleek and modern aesthetic along with durability and easy maintenance. Gypsum ceiling tiles are made from gypsum boards and provide both functional and decorative purposes with their versatility and ability to be easily molded into various shapes and designs.</p></p>
