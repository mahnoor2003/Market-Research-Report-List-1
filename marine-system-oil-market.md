<p><ul><li>Low Speed System Oil</li><li>Middle Speed System Oil</li><li>High Speed System Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine system oil serves a crucial role in lubricating and cooling the engines of marine vessels. It is categorized into three types: low speed system oil, middle speed system oil, and high speed system oil. Low speed system oil is designed for slow-speed engines, which operate below 400 RPM. Middle speed system oil is suitable for medium-speed engines that function between 400 and 1200 RPM. High speed system oil caters to high-speed engines, typically operating above 1200 RPM. Each type of marine system oil is specifically formulated to meet the unique requirements of its corresponding engine speed range.</p></p>
