<p><p>The medical grade silicone tubing and hose market comprises two main types: platinum cured silicone tubing & hose and peroxide cured silicone tubing & hose. Platinum cured silicone tubing & hose is made using a platinum catalyst, ensuring high purity and minimal risk of leaching. It is commonly used in applications requiring biocompatibility and is resistant to high temperatures and chemicals. On the other hand, peroxide cured silicone tubing & hose is made using a peroxide catalyst and is known for its lower cost and broader range of applications.</p></p>
