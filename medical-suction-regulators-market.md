<p><p>Medical suction regulators are devices used in hospitals and healthcare settings to control and maintain the level of suction during medical procedures. The two main types of suction regulators are continuous suction regulators and intermittent suction regulators. Continuous suction regulators provide a constant level of suction, while intermittent suction regulators deliver suction in cycles or intervals. Both types ensure effective removal of fluids, secretions, or air from the patient's body, thereby facilitating smooth medical procedures and patient care.</p></p>
