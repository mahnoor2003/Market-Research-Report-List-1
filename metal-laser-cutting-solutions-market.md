<p><p>Metal laser cutting solutions market types include laser vaporization cutting, laser melting cutting, laser oxygen cutting, and other markets. Laser vaporization cutting is a process where the laser beam heats the metal surface until it vaporizes, creating a cut. Laser melting cutting involves a high-intensity laser beam melting the metal surface to produce a cut. Laser oxygen cutting combines laser energy and oxygen to melt the metal and create a cut. Other market types refer to alternative methods or technologies used for metal cutting, such as water jet cutting or plasma cutting.</p></p>
