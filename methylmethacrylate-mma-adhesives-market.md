<p><p>Methylmethacrylate (MMA) Adhesives Market Types:</p><p>1. Thermosetting Adhesives: These adhesives undergo a chemical reaction when exposed to heat or certain curing agents, resulting in a permanent bonding. They possess excellent heat resistance, stability, and durability, making them ideal for applications requiring strong and long-lasting adhesion, such as automotive and aerospace industries.</p><p>2. Thermoplasticity Adhesives: These adhesives soften upon heating and solidify upon cooling, allowing for reversible bonding. They offer advantages like easy processing, reusability, and flexibility. Thermoplasticity adhesives find applications in areas where adjustable bonding or repetitive disassembly is required, such as electronics, packaging, and textiles.</p></p>
