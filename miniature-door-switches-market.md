Mechanical switches are traditional types that utilize physical contact to activate the switch when the door is opened or closed. On the other hand, non-contact switches work based on proximity or magnetic fields, requiring no physical touch between the switch and the door. These switches offer advantages like durability, reliability, and ease of installation, making them suitable for diverse industries such as automotive, aerospace, and home automation.</p></p>
