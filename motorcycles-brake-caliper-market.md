<p><p>The market for motorcycle brake calipers can be divided into two main types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers have a single cylinder and piston, providing a simple braking system with less stopping power. On the other hand, multi-piston calipers have multiple cylinders and pistons, allowing for better braking performance and more control. They are commonly used in high-performance motorcycles. These two types cater to different needs and preferences of motorcycle riders in terms of braking power and overall riding experience.</p></p>
