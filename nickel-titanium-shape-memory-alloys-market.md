<p><p>Nickel-Titanium Shape Memory Alloys Market consists of two types: High Temperature Austenite Phase and Low Temperature Martensite Phase. In the High Temperature Austenite Phase, the material exhibits a stable structure at higher temperatures. This phase provides excellent superelastic properties, making it suitable for applications requiring high elasticity and shape recovery. On the other hand, the Low Temperature Martensite Phase occurs at lower temperatures, allowing for shape memory properties. This phase is beneficial for applications where the material needs to retain a temporary shape and then revert to its original shape upon heating.</p></p>
