<p><p>The non-gravity massage chair market includes two types: Whole Body Zero Gravity and Half Body Zero Gravity chairs. Whole Body Zero Gravity chairs are designed to recline and distribute the user's weight evenly, reducing pressure on the spine and joints. This position promotes a sense of weightlessness and helps relieve muscle tension. On the other hand, Half Body Zero Gravity chairs allow the user to recline partially, providing targeted massage and relaxation to specific areas such as the back, neck, and shoulders. Both types aim to provide a more comfortable and therapeutic massage experience.</p></p>
