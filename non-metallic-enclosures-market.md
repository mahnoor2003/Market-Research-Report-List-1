<p><p>The non-metallic enclosures market comprises fiberglass and polycarbonate enclosures. Fiberglass enclosures are made from a composite material consisting of glass fibers and a resin matrix, making them lightweight, durable, and resistant to corrosion and electromagnetic interference. They are commonly used in industries requiring high levels of protection, such as oil and gas and marine applications. Polycarbonate enclosures, on the other hand, are made from a transparent thermoplastic material, offering excellent impact resistance, UV protection, and fire resistance. They find applications in electrical, automotive, and telecommunications industries.</p></p>
