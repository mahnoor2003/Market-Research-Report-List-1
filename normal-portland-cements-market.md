Standard consistency refers to the thickness of the cement paste, which is suitable for general construction purposes. It has a standardized water-to-cement ratio and is commonly used for various applications. On the other hand, extra thick consistency cement has a higher water requirement, resulting in a thicker paste. This type is specifically designed for certain construction needs that demand higher strength and reduced bleeding.
