<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Obstetrical Suction Pump Market can be classified into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I refers to manual suction pumps that require human effort to operate and generate suction pressure. These pumps usually have a simple design and are affordable in comparison to Type II pumps. On the other hand, Type II pumps are automated suction devices that utilize electricity or batteries to create the necessary suction pressure. They offer enhanced suction power, precision, and convenience, but are relatively more expensive than Type I pumps.</p></p>
