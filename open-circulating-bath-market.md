<p><p>Open circulating bath refers to a type of bath that is used in various industries and research applications. It can be divided into two market types: water bath and oil bath. Water baths use water as the circulating medium and are commonly used for general laboratory purposes, such as sample incubation or temperature-controlled reactions. Oil baths, on the other hand, use specialized oils as the circulating medium and are typically utilized in high-temperature applications or for heating flammable or volatile substances. Both market types provide controlled temperature conditions for efficient and safe experimentation.</p></p>
