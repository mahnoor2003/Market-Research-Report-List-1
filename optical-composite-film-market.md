<p><p>Optical Composite Film (OCF) is a type of film used in various electronic display devices to enhance optical properties. Different types of OCFs include DOP (Diffusion Optical Plastic), POP (Polarizer Optical Plastic), MOP (Micro Optical Plastic), POM (Polarizer Optical Material), and Others. DOP is designed to scatter light and diffuse it evenly, improving brightness and clarity. POP improves the polarizing capability of the display. MOP features microstructures to enhance viewing angles. POM is responsible for polarizing light in the display. The "Others" category encompasses any other types of OCFs that do not fall under the aforementioned classifications.</p></p>
