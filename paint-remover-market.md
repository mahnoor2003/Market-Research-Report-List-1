<p><ul><li>Solvent Type</li><li>Caustic Type</li><li>Acidic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paint remover market is broadly classified into three types: solvent type, caustic type, and acidic type. Solvent-based paint removers consist of organic solvents like acetone or mineral spirits. Caustic paint removers use strong alkaline substances, such as sodium hydroxide, to break down paint. Acidic paint removers utilize acids like hydrochloric or sulfuric acid to dissolve paint. Each type has its advantages and considerations. Solvent type is effective on various surfaces, caustic type works well on metals, while acidic type is mainly used for masonry or concrete surfaces.</p></p>
