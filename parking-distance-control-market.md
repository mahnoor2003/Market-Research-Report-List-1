<p><p>Parking Distance Control, also known as PDC or Parking Sensors, is an automotive safety feature that uses ultrasonic sensors to assist drivers in parking their vehicles safely. The sensors are typically mounted on the front and rear bumpers of the vehicle and emit sound waves that bounce off nearby obstacles. The system then calculates the distance between the vehicle and the obstacle and alerts the driver through audible or visual signals.</p><p>The global Parking Distance Control Market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and the growing emphasis on vehicle safety are driving the market growth. Additionally, the rising number of parking-related accidents and the need to reduce vehicle damage are further fueling the demand for parking distance control systems.</p><p>Market analysis suggests that the Parking Distance Control Market is segmented by type, vehicle type, and region.
