<p><p>The pharmaceutical industry utilizes various types of Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) for different purposes. Automated Forklift AGVs are designed to lift and move heavy loads, replacing traditional forklifts. Tugger AGVs operate by towing multiple carts or trailers in a train-like manner. These diverse AGV types provide flexibility and efficiency in different areas of pharmaceutical manufacturing and distribution processes.</p></p>
