<p><p>Phosphates for animal feed and nutrition are available in various forms such as dicalcium phosphates, monocalcium phosphates, mono-dicalcium phosphate, tricalcium phosphate, defluorinated phosphate, and others. Dicalcium phosphates are widely used as a source of phosphorus and calcium for animal growth. Monocalcium phosphates provide similar benefits but have higher digestibility. Mono-dicalcium phosphates are a combination of both and offer improved bioavailability of phosphorus. Tricalcium phosphate is rich in calcium and provides a slow-release supply of phosphorus. Defluorinated phosphate is a low-fluoride alternative. Other types of phosphates are also available in the market, offering specific advantages to meet animal nutrition requirements.</p></p>
