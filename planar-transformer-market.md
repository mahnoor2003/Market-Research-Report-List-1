<p><p>The planar transformer market includes various types such as PCB type transformer, thick film transformer, thin film transformer, and others. PCB type transformers are integrated on printed circuit boards, while thick film transformers use a thick film deposition process for high power applications. Thin film transformers, on the other hand, are fabricated using a thin film deposition process for low power applications. Other types of planar transformers may include hybrid transformers or miniaturized transformers, offering unique design and performance characteristics.</p></p>
