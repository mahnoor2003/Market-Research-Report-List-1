<p><p>The Platform Screen Door System market includes two types: Full Height and Half Height. Full Height platform screen doors are designed to cover the entire height of the platform, providing complete protection and preventing unauthorized access. They are commonly used in metro stations and high-speed railway platforms. On the other hand, Half Height platform screen doors are smaller in size and cover only half of the platform's height. They are usually installed in light rail transit and commuter railway stations, offering a lower-cost solution for crowd management and passenger safety.</p></p>
