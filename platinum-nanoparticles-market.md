<p><p>Platinum nanoparticles are particles of platinum that have a size range of 1-100 nanometers. Additionally, their applications in the electronics industry for semiconductors, sensors, and memory devices are also contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Moreover, the growing focus on renewable energy sources and the increasing adoption of fuel cell technology are further propelling the demand for platinum nanoparticles. These nanoparticles are widely used as catalysts in fuel cells for clean energy production.</p><p>Another key factor driving market growth is the rising application of platinum nanoparticles in the medical and healthcare sector.
