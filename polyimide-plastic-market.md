<p><strong>The Polyimide Plastic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting</li><li>Thermoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide plastic is a versatile material that exists in two market types: thermosetting and thermoplastic. Thermosetting polyimide plastics are formed through a chemical reaction that irreversibly cross-links the polymer chains, making them rigid and heat resistant. They cannot be melted or reformed without degradation. On the other hand, thermoplastic polyimide plastics can be melted and reshaped repeatedly without undergoing any chemical change. They offer high strength, flexibility, and heat resistance, making them suitable for various applications in industries such as aerospace, electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
