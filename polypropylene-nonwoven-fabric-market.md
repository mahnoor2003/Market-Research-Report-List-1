<p><strong>The Polypropylene Nonwoven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbonded Fabric</li><li>Staples Fabric</li><li>Melt Blown Fabric</li><li>Composite Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polypropylene nonwoven fabric market encompasses various fabric types. Spunbonded fabric is produced through melting and extrusion of polypropylene fibers, resulting in a strong and durable material commonly used in construction and agriculture. Staples fabric is made by mechanically interlocking short polypropylene fibers, offering high absorbency and softness, suitable for hygiene products like diapers. Melt-blown fabric involves a process where ultrafine polypropylene fibers are formed by melt-extrusion, leading to excellent filtration capabilities, utilized in medical masks. Composite fabric combines various fabric types to leverage their individual properties, finding applications in automotive, geotextiles, and other sectors.</p></p>
