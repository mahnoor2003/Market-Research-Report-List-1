<p><p>Polyurethane insulation boards are commonly used in the construction industry to provide effective thermal insulation. They are available in two main types: expanded polystyrene foam (EPS) and extruded polystyrene foam (XPS). EPS boards are made by expanding polystyrene beads, resulting in a lightweight and cost-effective insulation material. On the other hand, XPS boards are created by extruding molten polystyrene, making them denser, stronger, and more resistant to moisture. Both types offer excellent thermal insulation properties and are suitable for various construction applications.</p></p>
