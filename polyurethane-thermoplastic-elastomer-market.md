<p><p>Polyurethane Thermoplastic Elastomer (TPE) market is categorized into three types: polyester, polyether, and polycaprolactone. Polyester TPEs are known for their excellent mechanical properties and resistance to chemicals. They are widely used in automotive, footwear, and consumer goods industries. Polyether TPEs have exceptional resistance to water, hydrolysis, and low-temperature flexibility, making them suitable for applications in the medical, sports, and electronics sectors. Polycaprolactone TPEs offer high tear strength and resilience, making them ideal for industrial applications requiring durability and flexibility.</p></p>
