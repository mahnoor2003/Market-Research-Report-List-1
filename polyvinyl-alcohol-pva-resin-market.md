<p><p>Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) Resin, which is widely used in various industries, can be classified into two main types: industrial grade and medicine grade. Industrial grade PVA resin is primarily utilized in applications such as adhesives, coatings, films, and textile sizing due to its excellent adhesion properties and film-forming abilities. On the other hand, medicine grade PVA resin meets more stringent quality standards and is extensively used in pharmaceutical and medical applications, including drug delivery systems, wound dressings, and eye drops, owing to its high purity and biocompatibility.</p></p>
