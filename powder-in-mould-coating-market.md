<p><strong>The Powder In-Mould Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMC</li><li>BMC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder in-mould coating market types include Sheet Moulding Compound (SMC) and Bulk Moulding Compound (BMC). SMC is a high-performance material consisting of a thermoset resin, chopped glass fibers, and fillers. It is commonly used for manufacturing lightweight parts with excellent strength and mechanical properties. On the other hand, BMC is a thermosetting plastic material composed of resin, fibers, and fillers. It is used for molding intricate and complex shapes with superior electrical and thermal insulation properties. Both SMC and BMC offer advantages such as cost-effectiveness, design flexibility, and high productivity in various industries.</p></p>
