These therapies can be categorized into different types. Hormone therapy involves the use of medications that block or suppress hormones that promote cancer growth. Immunotherapies work by activating the patient's immune system to fight against cancer. Targeted therapy utilizes drugs that target specific abnormalities found in cancer cells. Monoclonal antibody therapy employs artificially created antibodies to specifically recognize and attack cancer cells. Gene therapy involves altering a patient's genes to enhance their ability to fight cancer.</p></p>
