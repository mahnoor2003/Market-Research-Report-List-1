<p><p>The pretreatment foam market consists of two main types: non-enzymatic and enzymatic. Non-enzymatic foam is a cleaning solution that does not contain enzymes as its active ingredients. It is effective in removing dirt, grime, and stains from various surfaces. Enzymatic foam, on the other hand, incorporates enzymes in its composition, which break down organic compounds for deeper cleaning. This type of foam is particularly useful in removing tough stains and eliminating foul odors. Both non-enzymatic and enzymatic foam offer solutions for effective pre-cleaning before various industrial processes.</p></p>
