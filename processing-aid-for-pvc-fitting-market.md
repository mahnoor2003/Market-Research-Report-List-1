<p><p>Processing aids are additives used in the manufacturing of PVC fittings to enhance their performance and processing properties. There are two types of processing aids commonly used: Prill and Powder. Prill processing aids are small spherical particles that offer better dispersion and easier handling during production. Powder processing aids, on the other hand, are fine particles that provide improved blending and homogeneity. Both types contribute to the smooth processing of PVC fittings, resulting in improved melt flow, reduction in processing time, and enhanced product quality.</p></p>
