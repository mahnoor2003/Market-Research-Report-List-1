The waveplate consists of a thin layer of crystalline quartz sandwiched between two parallel glass plates.</p><p>Quartz Crystal Waveplates find applications in various industries such as telecommunications, aerospace, medical, and semiconductors, among others. These waveplates provide high precision and are commonly used in optical communication and laser applications. They offer more flexibility and are often utilized in polarization controlling devices and modulators for optical systems.
