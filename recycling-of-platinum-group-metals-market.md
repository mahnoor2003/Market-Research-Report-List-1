<p><p>The recycling of platinum group metals (PGMs) refers to the process of recovering and reusing metals such as platinum, palladium, rhodium, ruthenium, iridium, and osmium from various sources. Recycling of PGMs helps in reducing the reliance on primary sources such as mining and also provides significant cost and energy savings.</p><p>The market analysis of the recycling of PGMs highlights its growing importance across industries. The increasing demand for PGMs, coupled with the limited availability of these metals, has led to an upsurge in the adoption of recycling methods.
