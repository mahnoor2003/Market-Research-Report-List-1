<p><ul><li>Mechanical Tests</li><li>Imaging Tests</li><li>In Vitro Diagnostics (IVD) Tests</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Respiratory Infectious Disease Diagnostics market is categorized into three types of tests: mechanical tests, imaging tests, and In Vitro Diagnostics (IVD) tests. Mechanical tests involve the measurement of lung function using devices such as spirometers and peak flow meters. Imaging tests utilize technologies like X-rays, CT scans, and MRI scans to visualize respiratory structures and identify abnormalities. In Vitro Diagnostic tests involve analyzing respiratory samples, such as sputum or blood, in a laboratory setting to detect specific pathogens or biomarkers associated with respiratory infections.</p></p>
