<p><ul><li>Logarithmic Detector</li><li>RMS Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF power detector for communication equipment market consists of two major types: logarithmic detector and RMS detector. A logarithmic detector measures the logarithm of the input power and provides an output voltage proportional to the input power level. This makes it suitable for a wide range of applications with varying power levels. On the other hand, an RMS detector calculates the root mean square of the input power and provides a DC output voltage that is proportional to the average power level. This type of detector is commonly used in applications that require accurate power measurements.</p></p>
