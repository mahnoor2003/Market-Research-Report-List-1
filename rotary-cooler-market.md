<p><p>A rotary cooler is a type of industrial equipment used for cooling materials in various manufacturing processes. As the drum rotates, the material being cooled is fed into the cooler and is exposed to ambient air or cool gases, which help decrease its temperature.</p><p>Market Analysis: The global rotary cooler market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The market is driven by increasing industrialization across various sectors such as cement, mining, chemicals, and food processing. In the cement industry, rotary coolers are widely used to cool the clinker formed during the cement manufacturing process. Rising demand for cement and infrastructure development projects in developing countries is fueling the growth of the rotary cooler market.</p><p>Furthermore, the mining industry is adopting rotary coolers to cool down the ore after various processing stages. <p><p>Rotary coolers refer to industrial devices used for cooling materials in various processes. There are two main types of rotary coolers: air rotary coolers and water rotary coolers.
