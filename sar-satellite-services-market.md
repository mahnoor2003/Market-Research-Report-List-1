<p><p>The SAR (Synthetic Aperture Radar) satellite services market is categorized into three main types: L-band, C-band, and X-band. </p><p>L-band SAR satellites operate at a lower frequency range, providing longer wavelengths for better penetration through clouds and vegetation. They are suitable for applications such as forestry monitoring and disaster management.</p><p>C-band SAR satellites operate at a slightly higher frequency range, offering medium wavelengths with good resolution capabilities. They are commonly used for applications like agriculture monitoring and maritime surveillance.</p><p>X-band SAR satellites operate at the highest frequency range, providing shorter wavelengths with very high-resolution imaging capabilities. They are predominantly used for applications such as military reconnaissance, infrastructure monitoring, and oil spill detection.</p></p>
