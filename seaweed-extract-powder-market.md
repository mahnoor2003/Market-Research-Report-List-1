Brown seaweed extract powder is obtained from brown algae, and is rich in fucoxanthin, which has antioxidant properties. Green seaweed extract powder is derived from green algae and is valued for its high chlorophyll content. These different types cater to various industries, including food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic.</p></p>
