<p><p>Self-healing paint protection film market is categorized into different types, namely PVC, PU, and TPU. PVC type film is made from polyvinyl chloride and offers excellent flexibility and durability. PU type film, made from polyurethane, provides enhanced scratch resistance and heat resistance compared to PVC. TPU type film, composed of thermoplastic polyurethane, combines the benefits of both PVC and PU films, offering superior flexibility, durability, and scratch resistance. These variations cater to different customer preferences and application needs within the self-healing paint protection film market.</p></p>
