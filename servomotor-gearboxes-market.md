<p><p>The servo motor gearboxes market encompasses different types of gearboxes, including linear servo gearboxes and right angle servo gearboxes. Linear servo gearboxes are designed to convert the rotational motion of the servo motor into linear motion, providing smooth and precise movement along a straight path. On the other hand, right angle servo gearboxes are intended for applications where the servo motor needs to transmit power at a 90-degree angle, allowing for efficient power transmission and flexibility in mounting positions. Both types of gearboxes cater to specific mechanical requirements and play a crucial role in enhancing the performance of servo motor systems.</p></p>
