Side glowing signage lighting is designed to emit light from the sides of the sign, creating a halo effect and emphasizing the shape of the signage. Back glowing signage lighting, on the other hand, illuminates the back of the sign, creating a glow that enhances the visibility of the text or graphics displayed on the sign. These different types of signage lighting cater to various aesthetic and visibility needs.</p></p>
