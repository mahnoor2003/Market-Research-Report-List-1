<p><ul><li>Water-soluble Type</li><li>Citrate-soluble Type</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Silicon fertilizer market is divided into two main types: water-soluble and citrate-soluble. Water-soluble silicon fertilizers can be easily dissolved in water and are readily absorbed by plants through their roots. They enhance plant growth, increase resistance to pests and diseases, and improve the overall quality of crops. On the other hand, citrate-soluble silicon fertilizers are designed to be soluble in citric acid-based solutions. They offer similar benefits as water-soluble types but are specifically formulated for certain crops or soil conditions.</p></p>
