<p><p>Single pipe expansion joints are flexible connectors used in piping systems to absorb thermal expansion, vibration, and misalignment. They allow the pipe to expand and contract without putting stress on the system, preventing damage to the piping, equipment, and surrounding structures. These joints are commonly used in industrial processes, power plants, HVAC systems, and other piping applications.</p><p>The single pipe expansion joints market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period.
