<p><p>Single-use bio-processing systems refer to disposable equipment and devices used in the biopharmaceutical industry. These include disposable bioreactors, mixers, containers, tubing, connectors, sampling systems, purification devices, columns, and probes/sensors. Disposable bioreactors provide a sterile environment for cell culture, while mixers and containers facilitate mixing and storage. Tubing and connectors allow fluid transfer, while sampling systems enable sample collection. Purification devices and columns assist in the separation and purification of bio-products. Probes/sensors provide real-time monitoring of various parameters during bio-processing. These systems offer convenience, cost-effectiveness, and minimized contamination risks.</p></p>
