<p><p>Skin resurfacing machines are used to improve the appearance and texture of the skin. There are two main types of skin resurfacing machines: ablative and non-ablative. Ablative machines remove the outer layers of skin, promoting collagen production and reducing the appearance of wrinkles, scars, and sun damage. Non-ablative machines, on the other hand, work by heating the deeper layers of skin without removing any outer layers. This stimulates collagen production and helps improve skin tone and texture. Both types of machines offer effective solutions for individuals looking to rejuvenate their skin.</p></p>
