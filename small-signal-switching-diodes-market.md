<p><p>The Small-Signal Switching Diodes market can be classified into three types based on voltage ratings: Below 50V, 50-100V, and Above 100V. Below 50V diodes are designed to handle low voltage circuits and are suitable for applications like signal processing and low-power switching. The 50-100V diodes are capable of handling intermediate voltage levels and are commonly used in medium-power digital electronics. On the other hand, Above 100V diodes are built to tolerate high voltage circuits and are commonly utilized in power conversion and industrial applications where high-voltage switching is required.</p></p>
