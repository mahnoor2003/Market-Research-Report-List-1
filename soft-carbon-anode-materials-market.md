<p><p>Soft carbon anode materials market types can be classified into three main categories: petroleum coke source, needle coke source, and others. Petroleum coke source refers to anode materials derived from the residual oil obtained from crude oil refining. Needle coke source pertains to anode materials produced from special petroleum-based feedstocks. Lastly, the "others" category includes anode materials derived from alternative sources such as coal tar pitch, mesophase pitch, and pitch blend. These various sources provide different qualities and characteristics to soft carbon anode materials, catering to diverse industry requirements.</p></p>
