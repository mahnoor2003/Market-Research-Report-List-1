<p><p>Solar panel adhesives are used to bond solar cells and encapsulate them within the panel structure. Two commonly used types of adhesives in this market are epoxy and polyurethane. Epoxy adhesives offer strong bonding properties and high temperature resistance, making them suitable for demanding solar panel applications. On the other hand, polyurethane adhesives provide excellent flexibility and durability, making them ideal for solar panel installations that may experience vibrations or movements. Both epoxy and polyurethane adhesives contribute to the efficient operation and longevity of solar panels.</p></p>
