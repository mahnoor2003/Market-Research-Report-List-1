<p><p>Special effect pigments, such as metallic pigments and pearlescent pigments, are used in various industries for their unique visual qualities. Metallic pigments, made from metal flakes, give surfaces a shiny, reflective appearance, adding depth and vibrancy. On the other hand, pearlescent pigments are composed of mica flakes coated with titanium dioxide, creating a shimmering effect similar to the iridescence of pearls. These pigments are widely used in automotive coatings, cosmetics, and printing inks to enhance the overall aesthetics and create eye-catching effects.</p></p>
