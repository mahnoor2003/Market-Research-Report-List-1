<p><p>Specialty cement refers to various types of specialized cement used in specific applications. Rapid Hardening Cement is designed to gain strength quickly, ideal for projects that require early curing. Low Thermal and Heat Cement has reduced heat generation during hydration, suitable for large concrete structures. Oil Well Cement is used in oil well drilling and completion. Expansive Cement expands when it reacts with water, useful for sealing gaps. Refractory Cement can withstand high temperatures, commonly used in furnaces and kilns. Color Cement offers a range of colored options for decorative purposes. Other specialty cements include those designed for underwater applications, sulfate resistance, and high durability.</p></p>
