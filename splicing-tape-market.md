<p><p>Splicing tape typically comes in two types based on the resin used and the backing material. Resin-based splicing tape is made from materials like acrylic, silicone, or rubber, which provide different levels of adhesion and temperature resistance. Backing material-based splicing tape can be made from materials like polyester, polypropylene, or paper, offering different levels of strength and flexibility. These variations in resin and backing material allow splicing tapes to be tailored for specific applications, such as joining films and papers in the printing or packaging industry.</p></p>
