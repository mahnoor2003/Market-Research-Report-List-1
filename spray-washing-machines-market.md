<p><ul><li>Top Loader Machine</li><li>Front Loader Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray washing machines are classified into two types: top loader machines and front loader machines. Top loader machines are designed with a vertical axis, where clothes are loaded and unloaded from the top of the machine. They are generally more affordable and easier to use. On the other hand, front loader machines have a horizontally positioned drum that is accessed from the front. They are known for their higher efficiency and ability to use less water and energy, although they can be pricier and require more maintenance.</p></p>
