<p><strong>The Stationary Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive-Displacement Compressors</li><li>Centrifuga Compressors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary compressors are classified into three main types: positive-displacement compressors, centrifugal compressors, and other market compressors. </p><p>Positive-displacement compressors operate by trapping air in a specific volume and then decreasing the volume to compress the air. They are commonly used in applications requiring high pressure levels and are known for their reliability and efficiency.</p><p>Centrifugal compressors, on the other hand, utilize rotating impellers to accelerate the airflow, creating high kinetic energy. They are suitable for applications that require high flow rates and are often used in large-scale industrial processes.</p><p>The "other market" category encompasses compressors that do not fall under the positive-displacement or centrifugal types. This includes niche compressors designed for specific industries or specialized applications.</p></p>
