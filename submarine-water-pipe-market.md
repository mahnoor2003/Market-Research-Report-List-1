<p><p>The submarine water pipe market can be divided into two main types: Shallow Water Pipe and Deep Water Pipe. Shallow Water Pipe refers to the installation of water pipes in relatively shallow ocean depths. It is often used for nearshore projects or in areas with less challenging conditions. On the other hand, Deep Water Pipe refers to the installation of water pipes in much deeper ocean depths, often required for offshore projects or in areas with more complex conditions. These market types reflect the different requirements and challenges associated with water pipe installation in various ocean depths.</p></p>
