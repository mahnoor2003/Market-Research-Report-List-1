<p><ul><li>Polysulfide</li><li>Mercapto</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sulfur-containing silane market comprises various types, namely polysulfide, mercapto, and others. Polysulfide silanes are characterized by the presence of multiple sulfur atoms in their structure, enhancing their cross-linking properties and making them suitable for applications like sealants and adhesives. Mercapto silanes, on the other hand, contain sulfur and functional groups like thiol, enabling them to provide enhanced adhesion properties in applications such as rubber and plastics. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses sulfur-containing silanes with unique structures and properties, typically utilized in specialized applications.</p></p>
