<p><p>The Synthetic Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) market consists of various types such as Styrenic Block Copolymers (SBC), Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU), Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV), Thermoplastic Polyvinyl Chloride (TPVC), and Others. SBC TPEs offer excellent elasticity and oil resistance, making them ideal for applications like footwear and adhesives. TPU TPEs provide exceptional mechanical properties, high tensile strength, and tear resistance, suitable for automotive components and wire coatings. TPV TPEs combine advantages of elastomers and plastics, making them useful in automotive weather seals. TPVC TPEs offer flexibility and flame retardancy, making them applicable to wire insulation. Other types of TPEs include TPO and COPE, each with unique characteristics for specific applications.</p></p>
