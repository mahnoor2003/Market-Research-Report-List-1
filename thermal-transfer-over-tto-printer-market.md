<p><p>The Thermal Transfer Over (TTO) printer market consists of two primary market types: Single Head and Multi Head. Single Head TTO printers utilize a single printhead, making them suitable for applications where a single line of code or small message is required. On the other hand, Multi Head TTO printers feature multiple printheads, allowing for the printing of larger codes or multiple lines simultaneously. These printers are ideal for applications that demand higher levels of coding and labeling flexibility, efficiency, and speed.</p></p>
