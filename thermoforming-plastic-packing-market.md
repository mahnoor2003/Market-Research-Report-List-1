<p><strong>The Thermoforming Plastic Packing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Formed</li><li>Pressure Formed</li><li>Mechanical Formed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoforming plastic packaging market comprises three main types: vacuum formed, pressure formed, and mechanical formed packaging. Vacuum formed packaging involves heating a plastic sheet and shaping it using vacuum pressure to form the desired packaging. Pressure formed packaging involves applying pressure to the heated plastic sheet to achieve the desired shape. Mechanical formed packaging utilizes mechanical force, such as stamping or pressing, to shape the plastic sheet. These methods offer different benefits and are commonly used in various industries for packaging products efficiently and securely.</p></p>
