Polyester composites exhibit high strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals. Epoxy composites offer excellent bonding properties and are widely used in aerospace and automotive applications. Vinyl ester composites provide good corrosion resistance and are commonly used in marine and chemical industries. Phenolic composites possess flame retardant properties and find applications in construction and electrical sectors. Polyurethane composites offer flexibility and impact resistance, suitable for automotive and consumer goods.
