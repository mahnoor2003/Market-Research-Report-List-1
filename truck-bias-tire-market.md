<p><p>The truck bias tire market includes two main types: ordinary bias tires and bias belted tires. Ordinary bias tires are constructed with multiple layers of rubberized plies running diagonally across the tire. They are known for their strong sidewalls and can handle heavy loads. On the other hand, bias belted tires consist of additional layers of belts made of steel or other synthetic materials, enhancing their durability and tread life. These tires offer improved stability and fuel efficiency while maintaining their load-carrying capacity.</p></p>
