<p><p>The truck rear retarder market consists of two main types: electric retarders and hydraulic retarders. Electric retarders use electrical energy to generate resistance and slow down the truck, thus reducing wear and tear on the brakes. They are more efficient and responsive, making them ideal for urban driving conditions. On the other hand, hydraulic retarders use fluid resistance to decelerate the truck. They are more suitable for long-distance driving on steep slopes, offering better heat dissipation and higher braking torque. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing truck safety and reducing brake maintenance costs.</p></p>
