<p><p>The two-wheeler position sensor market offers various types of sensors, including capacitive, magnetic, infrared (IR), force sensor, and others. Infrared (IR) sensors use infrared light reflections to gauge the position accurately. Force sensors can measure the force applied to gauge position.
