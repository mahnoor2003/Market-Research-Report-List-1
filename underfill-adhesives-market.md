<p><ul><li>Single Component Underfill Adhesives</li><li>Two-Component Underfill Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfill adhesives are used in electronic packaging to fill gaps between a chip and its substrate. Single component underfill adhesives are pre-mixed and ready to use, requiring no additional mixing or curing agents. They offer ease of use and faster production times. Two-component underfill adhesives consist of a base and a curing agent that need to be mixed before use. They provide stronger bonding properties and are commonly used in applications that require high thermal and mechanical stability.</p></p>
