<p><p>The U.V. cure resins market is divided into two types: Non-Acrylated and Oligoamines, and Acrylated Oligomers. Non-Acrylated and Oligoamines are a type of U.V. cure resins that do not contain any acrylate functional groups. They are commonly used in applications where acrylates are not suitable, such as inks, coatings, and adhesives. On the other hand, Acrylated Oligomers are U.V. cure resins that contain acrylate functional groups. They are widely used in various industries, including automotive, electronics, and packaging, for their excellent properties such as high reactivity and good adhesion.</p></p>
