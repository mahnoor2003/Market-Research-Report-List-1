<p><p>UV curable resin is a type of coating that cures when exposed to ultraviolet light. There are two main types of UV curable resins: solventborne UV resins and waterborne UV resins. Solventborne UV resins are based on solvent systems and offer excellent adhesion and durability. However, they have high environmental impact due to the release of volatile organic compounds. Waterborne UV resins, on the other hand, are water-based and have low VOC emissions, making them more environmentally friendly. They also provide good adhesion and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
