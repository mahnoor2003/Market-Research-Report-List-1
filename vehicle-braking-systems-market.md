<p><p>Vehicle braking systems are crucial for ensuring safe and efficient stopping of vehicles. Drum brakes and disc brakes are the two main types available in the market. Drum brakes are older technology and consist of a drum-shaped housing with brake shoes that expand when pressed against the inside of the drum. On the other hand, disc brakes utilize a flat rotor and calipers to squeeze brake pads against the rotor for braking. Disc brakes offer better performance, heat dissipation, and are more commonly used in modern vehicles.</p></p>
