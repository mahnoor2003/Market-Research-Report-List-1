<p><p>Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) Equipment refers to technology used in optical fiber networks to transmit multiple data signals simultaneously over a single fiber by leveraging different wavelengths of light. The market includes CWDM (Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexing) Equipment and DWDM (Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing) Equipment. CWDM Equipment utilizes wider wavelength spacing to transmit up to 18 channels over shorter distances, making it cost-effective for short-haul applications. On the other hand, DWDM Equipment enables the transmission of numerous channels over long distances, with narrow spacing and higher capacity, suitable for long-haul and high-capacity networks.</p></p>
