<p><p>Welding flux is classified into three categories: organic, inorganic, and resin market types. Organic welding flux is typically made from natural substances, such as wood flour or cellulose. Inorganic flux, on the other hand, is derived from minerals and metal oxides and provides excellent protection against impurities. Lastly, resin flux is a type of organic flux that contains thermoplastic resin, which melts during welding to form a protective layer. These different types of welding flux offer varying benefits and are chosen based on the specific welding requirements.</p></p>
