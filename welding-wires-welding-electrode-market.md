<p><p>Welding wires and welding electrodes are essential consumables used in various welding processes. Welding wires are solid metal wires that are fed through welding guns to provide the required filler material for welding. They are available in different materials such as carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and others, depending on the application requirement. Welding electrodes, on the other hand, are coated metal rods that are utilized in shielded metal arc welding (SMAW) or stick welding. They provide the arc necessary for melting the base metals together.</p><p>The welding wires and welding electrodes market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years. Factors driving this growth include the increasing adoption of welding in the construction and automotive industries, infrastructure development, and growing demand for energy and power sectors.
