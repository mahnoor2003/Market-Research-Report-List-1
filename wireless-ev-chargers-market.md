<p><p>The wireless EV chargers market comprises three main types: base charging pad, power control unit, and vehicle charging pad. The base charging pad acts as the foundation of the wireless charging system, transferring energy to the power control unit. The power control unit manages the charging process and regulates the power flow from the base charging pad to the vehicle charging pad. Lastly, the vehicle charging pad receives the energy and transfers it to the electric vehicle's battery, ensuring efficient and convenient charging. These three components work together to facilitate wireless charging for electric vehicles.</p></p>
