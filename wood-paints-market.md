<p><p>Wood paints market can be classified into two main types: waterborne and oil-based paints. Waterborne paints, often referred to as latex or acrylic paints, are made by suspending pigment particles in water. They are environmentally friendly, with low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and dry quickly. On the other hand, oil-based paints are made by suspending pigment particles in an oil medium, usually linseed oil. They provide a durable and glossy finish but require longer drying times and often contain more VOCs.</p></p>
