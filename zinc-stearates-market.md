<p><p>The Zinc Stearates market consists of two types: Solid Zinc Stearate and Aqueous Zinc Stearate. Solid Zinc Stearate is a fine white powder that is widely used as a lubricant and release agent in various industries such as plastics, rubber, and paints. It provides excellent heat stability and helps in preventing caking and adhesion. On the other hand, Aqueous Zinc Stearate is a water-based suspension of zinc stearate, primarily used in the cosmetics and personal care industry as an emulsifier and thickener. It offers good stability and ease of formulation.</p></p>
